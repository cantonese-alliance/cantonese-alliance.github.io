About the Cantonese Alliance of North America

Mission

  • Connect Cantonese instructors and organizations to preserve and promote the Cantonese language.

Overall Goal

  • Create a nonprofit that will raise money to fund advocacy, material development, workshops for instructors, tutor-led small-group classes, and research on the development of Cantonese as a heritage language.

Plan

  • Reach out to Cantonese teachers, content creators, community schools, and organizations
  • Sort out existing materials and identify unmet needs
  • Create materials to meet those needs. Examples:
    • Proficiency guidelines
    • Curriculum development
    • Materials for advanced learners
  • Form a core group of member organizations
  • Set up websites for members to access materials, e.g., Learn Cantonese in a Relaxing Way
  • Raise funds on GoFundMe in Spring 2022 for:
    • Developing further materials
    • Conducting workshops for Cantonese tutors and instructors
    • Supporting efforts to start/continue Cantonese conversation groups and community schools
    • Offering low-cost small-group Cantonese classes
    • Making grants available to member organizations and others
