Cantonese Classes, Certification, and Other Support in the SF Bay Area

  • Bilingual Authorization in Cantonese with Multiple Subject Teaching Credential, California
    • Graduate College of Education, San Francisco State University
  • Cantonese-English Bilingual Programs offered by San Francisco Public Schools
    • San Francisco Unified School District
      • Language Assessment Tests for Cantonese and other languages
      • Over 75% of all of the Chinese children served by this school district are Cantonese speakers.
  • Cantonese Classes Offered by Community Colleges and Other Public Schools
    • City College of San Francisco
    • College of Alameda
      • According to their college catalog: "The Cantonese Program at COA provides courses that satisfy Foreign Language requirements at 4-year colleges and universities, provides marketable foreign language skills and offers cultural enrichment."
    • Oakland High School
  • Cantonese Classes Offered by Community Organizations
    • 育才中文學校 American Chinese School
    • 西谷中文學校 West Valley Chinese Language School
    • 親子書坊 Books and Me
    • 博愛中文學校 Berryessa Chinese School
  • Cantonese at UC Berkeley
    • Chinese 3A and 3B: Elementary Cantonese, 4 units/semester
    • Chinese 3X: Elementary Cantonese for Heritage Speakers, 4 units
  • Cantonese at San Jose State University
    • Cantonese can be used to fulfill Chinese Majors' second world language requirement: "Chinese Majors must also take one year of a second world language, ancient or modern (or the equivalent; conversational Cantonese fulfills this requirement). Have your counselor check with the SJSU Department of World Languages and Literatures for approval of second language."
  • Cantonese Storytime
