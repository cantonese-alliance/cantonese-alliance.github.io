People

Coordinator

Hello, I am Sik Lee Dennig or Jēung Sehk-leih (張錫莉) in Cantonese. I was born in Hong Kong to parents from Eastern China. I grew up in a multilingual environment in which Cantonese mingled with different varieties of the Wu language (吳語). I hold a master’s degree in Psycholinguistics from the University of Alberta, Canada and a Ph.D. in Educational Linguistics from Stanford University, USA. I have taught language and linguistics courses in Hong Kong, Japan, Canada, and the U.S. The one that I'm most passionate about is Cantonese. Preserving Cantonese has become my lifelong mission..
